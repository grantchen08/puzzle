How to Play

The goal is to get the highest score by merging tiles.

  • Use arrow keys or swipe (or click-and-drag with a mouse on desktop) to move all tiles.
  • When two identical tiles touch, they merge into a new one.
  • A new tile appears after every move.
  • The game ends when the board is full and no moves are left.
